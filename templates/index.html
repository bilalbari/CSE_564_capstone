The dataset utilized in this visualization project is compiled from multiple sources to create a comprehensive dataset having 550+ companies.
● PatentsView Dataset: This forms the primary dataset and provides information about patents by companies. Contains data related to patents by companies.
● US Patent Citation Dataset: This dataset offers information on various types of citations received by companies. It is sourced from the US Patent Office.
● Market Data: Additional market data for companies was scraped from Nasdaq's historical records to understand their current market standing.
S.no. Attribute Description
1 Symbol Symbol of the company
2 Name Name of the company
3 Applicant The legal entity applying for patents
4 Last Sale Last sale stock price
5 Market Cap Total market capitalization
6 Country Headquarters of the company
7 IPO_Year Year of Initial Public Offering (IPO)
8 Volume Volume of trading
9 Sector Sector of the company
10 Industry Industry of the company
11 patents Number of patents
12 citations Total citations
13 FamilyCitations Citations from family members
14 NonFamilyCitations Non-family citations
15 P01-P21, C01-C21, NFC01-NFC21 Year-wise breakdown of patents, citations, and non-family citations respectively from 2001 to 2021
